Pattern Collections

Rivi's hand-drawn pattern is characteristic of the
Bouroullecs' uniqe design process.

Following in the footsteps of work by designers
such as Aino and Alvar Aalto, and Kaj Franck, Rivi
is subtle and as precise as it is imperfect.

Siena textile pattern created by Alvar Aalto which was
even named after the Italian city he adored.

Legend has it that the inspiration came from Siena's
medieval cathedral on Piazza del Duomo.

Available in a variety of colours derived from Aalto's
material world, it comes as loose fabric in different
cotton qualities or printed on a selection of charming
accessories.

Zebra is one of Artek's earliest and still most
famousetextiles.
It was most likely inspired by a zebra-patterned
textile that Aino Aalto purchased from the Zurich
design emporium Wohnbedarf in 1935
